This video is full focused on the various drift cars and drifters that took part in this year's edition of the Goodwood Festival of Speed. A part from the typical show runs on the hillclimb course, there were also some 'Driftkhana' competitive runs. During this competition some barrels with proximity sensors are placed along the course. The drivers have to brush some of them with the rear of their cars (without hitting them) or doing donuts around others. Hope you like it!
