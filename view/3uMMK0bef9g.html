How Significant is MotoGP Title for Bagnaia and Ducati? Full Analysis!

The country which live and breathe motorsport has found its another sporting hero. And looking from another side, bagnaia is already the ducati legend after clinching title in 15 long years. and like this, there are millions of upsides from bagnaia's title triumph. so, we will try to analyse: how significant this title is to bagnaia and also ducati? so here we go.

Pecco is already the national hero of italy. he has joined greats like agostini, rossi as the celebrated italian world champions in moto gp. for this, he will always be remembered. the country that lived and breathed motorsport both in moto gp and f1, success hasn't come easy for them. they were always here and there, but consistency wasn't there. so, the bike that they have right now, they look invincible at the moment. also, since 2009, italy didn't see it's champion except valentino rossi. hence, joining greats like them is already a feat for bagnaia. to fill in the shoes of someone like rossi was a huge task for pecco. and of course, he did it as calmly as possible. to ovecome 91 points deficit, it takes courage and composure which pecco had more than ever. whether he will be able to catch rossi is another question for other day but italy now will sing pecco. that's for sure.

Not only due to the fact that, this is ducati's 1st rider title in 15 years, there's a strong arguements to why bagnaia is already the ducati legend. yess, there absolutely is. this is the same team which was able to attract world champion like jorge lorenzo and valentino rossi and both failed miserably: especially rossi. this is the same team, that irritated their champion casey stoner towards the back end of 2010. and from then on, its been inconsistent ride for ducati. so they went in another direction in grooming italian riders alongside existing talent like andrea dovisioso in developing the bike. In doing so, they found pecco bagnaia from VR 46 academy. and ever since pecco is in factory ducati, its been uphill for them and they together have achieved the ultimate prize in moto gp: the rider title after 15 years and triple crown for the 1st time in their history. So, what we can say is: the trust towards ducati is back. and it's back big time.. so, for this, bagnaia will always be a ducati legend and who knows, this could be the beginning of new greatness from bagnaia and domination of ducati. to be able to overhaul the biggest gap in points tally, it was of course special ride from calm and composed pecco. there's no two ways about this.

It is ducati's 1st rider title in 15 years. so, this is much more than just special one for them. From a team point of view, ducati couldn't have asked for more: as they achieved the triple crown for the 1st time in their history and 2nd constructors championship in a row. now if we pour in deep, in recent years, the moto gp has been heavily dominated by japanese success of yamaha and honda. So, the bike ducati's have developed especially in 2022, now we could be seeing the shift of power from japanese to european manufacturers. Also, the development war, which has always betrayed ducati in the past, now seems to have resolved as they just got stronger and stronger towards the business end. So, this may mark the shift of power from japanese to italian manufacturers with aprilia also battling at the sharp end of the grid. in fact, this is great for moto gp in every sense to be honest.
